## 001 — Coinbase (Crypto Exchange, Marketing & Dashboard)

- **Category:** fintech
- **Audience register:** functional
- **Platform:** web
- **Color temperature:** neutral
- **Typography weight:** medium
- **Component density:** medium
- **Notable patterns:** hero with email capture CTA, mega-nav dropdown with product grid, asset price table with sparklines, search-as-you-type asset explorer, individual asset detail page with price chart, editorial learn/tutorial pages with illustrated cards, dark promotional landing section, wallet product page with feature list
- **URL:** https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Accel3-Repo/ui-lib/main/screenshots/001_coinbase.png
- **Style summary:** Institutional white with blue primary actions — Coinbase presents crypto with the visual language of a regulated financial platform, not a speculative exchange. Data tables and charts dominate; illustration is used for education, not decoration. Signals: the crypto on-ramp for people who find the space intimidating.
